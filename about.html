About
What is Local Loop?
Local Loop was created to help Greenville residents and visitors easily discover and share community events, special deals, and activities. Our goal is to make connecting with your community simple and enjoyable.
Anyone can post events that follow our guidelines. Browse events by tags, date, or location, and click "I’m interested" to receive an email reminder before an event starts. Local Loop is designed to be simple, fast, and accessible to everyone in the community.
About the developer
Hi, I’m Isaac. I developed LocalLoop as my senior project to help bring the Greenville community closer together. I built this platform using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Node.js. My goal with LocalLoop was to make it easy for anyone to discover and share local event, while keeping the experience simple and user-friendly.
