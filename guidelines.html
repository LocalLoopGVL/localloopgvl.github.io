Guidelines

  1. Be Relevant: This is a place for events, deals, and community gatherings.
  2. Be Clear: Be sure to tell people about your event in its description. Try to keep it short but informative.
  3. Be Respectful: No offensive, harmful, or discriminatory content.
  4. No Spam: Try to avoid repeated posts or unrelated promotions.
  5. Check Details: Make sure all times and locations are correct before posting.
Failure to comply with these guidelines will result in a post deletion.
