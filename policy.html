Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 12, 2026
Your privacy is extremely important to us. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.
Information We Collect
Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:
• Your email address
• Your password (stored in hashed form)
Your password is never stored in plain text. It is securely hashed before being saved.
Event Information
When you post an event, we store the information you provide, such as the event name, description, host name, and address. This information is publicly visible to other users. No other information is collected that you don't provide.
Miscellaneous
We may use certain browser-provided features to improve your experience on the platform. This includes:
• Preferred Theme
• Local Storage
This information is stored locally in your browser and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics services.
How Your Information is Used
We use collected data to:
• Create and manage user accounts
• Securely authenticate users
• Display events you post
• Maintain and improve the platform
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We process your information to provide the services you request and to operate the platform. This platform is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
Your Data
Data Storage
Your information is stored securely on our server. This includes event information, account information, and notify lists. Passwords are hashed using secure methods. Event data may be checked to maintain a safe and stable environment. We retain your information for as long as your account remains active.
Data Security
We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including:
• Password hashing
• Server-side authentication
• Secure data handling practices
No system can guarantee 100% security, but we try our absolute best. All data is stored on privately controlled infrastructure operated directly by us. We use a secure tunneling service to make the platform accessible over the internet. This service transmits encrypted traffic between users and our server and only processes data as necessary to route secure connections. If we become aware of a security breach affecting your personal information, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.
User Rights
You may request to:
• Access your stored account information
• Correct inaccurate information
• Delete your account and associated data
When your account is deleted, your personal information will be permanently removed from our systems. To make a request, please contact us.
Public vs Private

 Private Information
• Email addresses

• Other account information
Changes & Support
Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy as the platform evolves. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
Contact
If you have any questions about your privacy or data, please feel free to contact us.
